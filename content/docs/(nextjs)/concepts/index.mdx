Journium is built around the concept of an **Application**. 
An application in Journium  mirrors your own application and is the starting point for all your telemetry.

<DocsCategory />

## Concepts

### What is a Collection?

A Collection is a group of events that are related to a specific user or session.

### What is a Session?

A Session is a group of events that are related to a specific user or session.
