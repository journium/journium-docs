## What are Insights?

Insights are the output of Journium. They are generated by insight trackers and are displayed in the Journium dashboard.

## How are Insights Generated?

Insights are generated by insight trackers. Insight trackers are configured in the Journium dashboard and are used to track insights in the app.

## How are Insights Displayed?

Insights are displayed in the Journium dashboard. They are displayed in a list of insights with a summary of the data that was tracked in the app.
